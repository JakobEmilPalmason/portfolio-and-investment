# Compact Checklist — ODFL

**Date:** 2026-03-18

1. **Business**: ODFL operates a national less-than-truckload network of 260 service centers, consolidating partial shipments for over 50,000 industrial and retail customers with no single client exceeding 5.4% of revenue.
2. **Moat**: A structural cost advantage from 92% real estate ownership combined with 16 consecutive years as the #1 rated LTL carrier for quality (0.1% claims ratio) creates a premium service flywheel that rivals cannot match.
3. **Economics**: The company maintains a 10-year average ROIC of 26% and generated $955M in free cash flow (93% conversion) in FY2025 despite a 13% revenue decline from peak.
4. **Management**: Led by a 30-year veteran CEO and the founding Congdon family (9.8% insider ownership), management has retired 4.6% of shares since mid-2023 while remaining virtually debt-free.
5. **Debt risk**: ODFL holds a net cash position of $80M and 4,600x interest coverage, ensuring it can remain profitable and self-funding even if revenue were to drop 30% for two years.
6. **Price**: Today's 37.8x trailing P/E requires the freight cycle to inflect by late 2026 and margins to return to 29%+ just to justify the current $182 share price.
7. **Margin of safety**: The business is an impenetrable fortress, but the stock lacks any price protection as it trades 46% above its $125 base-case intrinsic value.
8. **Thesis breaker**: A permanent deterioration of the operating ratio above 80% or a departure from the disciplined debt-free capital allocation strategy would signal a broken competitive advantage.
