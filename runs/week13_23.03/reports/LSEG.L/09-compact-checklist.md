# Compact Checklist — LSEG.L

**Date:** 2026-03-29

1. **Business**: LSEG sells financial data subscriptions, index licensing, and clearing/trading infrastructure to thousands of global financial institutions, with ~70% recurring revenue and 90%+ retention rates that make cancellation operationally painful.
2. **Moat**: LCH clears >90% of global interest rate swaps (a regulatory-mandated near-monopoly), FTSE Russell indices are embedded in trillions of passive AUM, and 33+ petabytes of proprietary data create switching costs that no competitor can replicate quickly.
3. **Economics**: 88% gross margins, 50.3% EBITDA margins expanding 150bps/year, and 2x operating leverage (7.1% revenue growth converting to 15.7% EPS growth) confirm this is a capital-light compounder generating GBP 2.4B in equity free cash flow.
4. **Management**: Schwimmer delivered Refinitiv synergies ahead of target and compounded EPS at 15%+, but owns only 0.019% of shares, took a 46% pay raise that triggered a shareholder revolt, and accelerated buybacks to GBP 3B under Elliott activist pressure.
5. **Debt risk**: At 1.8x net debt/EBITDA with A3 credit rating and 9.6x adjusted interest coverage, LSEG would remain solvent and FCF-positive even in a 30% revenue decline scenario, though rising absolute debt (GBP 8.3B net) funding buybacks deserves monitoring.
6. **Price**: At 8,246 GBp (15.5x forward P/E vs peers at ~28x), the market must believe organic growth permanently stalls below 5% and margins stop expanding -- assumptions contradicted by every segment growing and EBITDA margins hitting record highs.
7. **Margin of safety**: The 13% price discount to bear-case IV of 7,200 GBp is thin by Buffett standards, but the business-level protection is exceptional -- 70% recurring revenue, deep switching costs, and 88% gross margins mean earnings hold up even in severe downturns.
8. **Thesis breaker**: Sell if organic revenue growth turns negative for two consecutive quarters, signaling that AI disintermediation or structural data commoditization is destroying the franchise rather than the temporary Q4 2026 revenue warning.
