# Compact Checklist — MCO

**Date:** 2026-03-12

1. **Business**: Moody's operates two synergistic segments — MIS (credit ratings, $4.1B revenue, 63.6% adjusted margin) and MA (subscription analytics, $3.6B revenue, 97% recurring) — forming a toll-booth on global capital flows with a two-minute pitch.
2. **Moat**: The NRSRO regulatory designation creates a legally enforced oligopoly where Moody's and S&P collectively rate 80%+ of the world's rated debt, backed by 125 years of irreproducible default history and a self-reinforcing issuer-investor network effect.
3. **Economics**: 29% ROIC, 51.1% consolidated adjusted operating margin, 105% FCF-to-net-income conversion, and 4% capex intensity in FY2025 — with incremental MIS revenue flowing almost entirely to the bottom line due to a largely fixed cost structure.
4. **Management**: CEO Rob Fauber's "connected intelligence" strategy produced $7.72B revenue (+9%), $14.94 adjusted EPS (+20%), and $2.6B FCF in FY2025, supported by 17 consecutive years of dividend growth and a $2.0B 2026 buyback commitment, with Berkshire Hathaway holding 14.54% as a permanent governance anchor.
5. **Debt risk**: Net debt of ~$5.0B is manageable at ~1.9x EBITDA with 15–17x interest coverage, $3.6B in liquidity, and stress-case FCF of $1.0–1.3B still comfortably covering the $220M interest and $700M dividend obligation.
6. **Price**: At ~$466, MCO trades at ~31x trailing adjusted EPS and ~28x forward EPS ($16.70 guidance midpoint), near base-case intrinsic value of $450–490, with an FCF yield of only ~2.8–3.0% — a fair but not discounted price.
7. **Margin of safety**: There is no price margin of safety at $466 — the 25/50/25 weighted-probability expected value is approximately $466, implying zero expected gain; a genuine margin of safety requires entry near $380–400.
8. **Thesis breaker**: A legislative mandate forcing an investor-pays model (5–10% probability) would collapse MIS margins from 63% to 30–40% and cut MIS revenue 40–60%, permanently impairing the business economics that underpin the entire thesis.
