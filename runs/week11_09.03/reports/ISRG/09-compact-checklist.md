# Compact Checklist — ISRG

**Date:** 2026-03-12

1. **Business**: Intuitive Surgical sells robotic surgery systems to hospitals for $1.5-2.5M each, then earns ~75-80% of its $10.1B revenue from single-use instruments and service contracts consumed with every one of the 3.15 million annual procedures — a razor/blade annuity that grows automatically as the 11,106-system installed base expands.
2. **Moat**: Surgeon training curves of 150-250 cases, hospital OR reconfigurations, 25 years of clinical data across thousands of peer-reviewed studies, and ~80% global market share create switching costs so severe that no competitor has taken meaningful share in two decades.
3. **Economics**: ROIC of 27%, gross margins of 66-69%, $2.5B in free cash flow (nearly doubled YoY), and revenue compounding at 15% since 2018 confirm this is one of the highest-quality economic engines in medtech.
4. **Management**: New CEO Dave Rosa (employee #9, 29-year veteran) inherited a zero-debt, $3.4B-cash balance sheet built through disciplined organic R&D investment and zero destructive acquisitions — though buybacks only barely offset SBC dilution at 0.4% net share reduction.
5. **Debt risk**: Zero debt, $5.9B in cash and investments, a 4.87x current ratio, and $2.5B annual FCF mean this company could survive a multi-year revenue collapse without raising a dollar of external capital.
6. **Price**: At ~$490 (62-67x trailing earnings), the market demands ~16-18% annual earnings growth for 5+ years, and the probability-weighted intrinsic value of ~$530 offers only 8% upside — this is a fair price for a great business, not a bargain.
7. **Margin of safety**: The business itself is nearly un-killable (zero debt, 80% recurring revenue, dominant share), but price margin of safety is absent — a Buffett-style 30% discount requires a pullback to ~$370.
8. **Thesis breaker**: Sell if quarterly da Vinci procedure growth drops below 8% for two consecutive quarters or if Medtronic Hugo captures more than 15% of new U.S. system placements for two consecutive years.
