## Schema Improvement Suggestions

1. **`last_analyzed_date`** — Date field for when the FINAL-REPORT was written. Currently I had to check directory names manually. A date field would enable automatic refresh_at calculation.

2. **`valuation_percentile`** — Replace `valuation_score` (0-10) with a field indicating where current valuation sits vs. the company's own 5-year range (1=cheapest, 5=most expensive). Would reduce the "quality 9 but valuation 5" ambiguity.

3. **`moat_type`** — Enum: `network_effects | switching_costs | cost_advantage | intangible_asset | efficient_scale`. Enables cross-portfolio moat diversification analysis without reading prose.

4. **`circle_of_competence`** — Boolean or 1-5 score for analyst familiarity with the business model. Currently embedded in `confidence` but conflated with data quality confidence. Separating them would improve override audit trails.
