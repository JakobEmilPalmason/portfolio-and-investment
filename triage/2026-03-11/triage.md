## Schema Improvement Suggestions

1. **`price_vs_52wk_range` (float, 0–1)** — Where the stock sits within its 52-week range. Currently captured only in b1_reason text; making it a field would enable systematic entry-setup filtering.
2. **`last_report_age_days` (integer)** — Days since last FINAL-REPORT.md. Currently assessed manually; a field would automate refresh vs. monitor logic.
3. **`covered_by` (string, nullable)** — Ticker of another name this cycle whose analysis covers this business (e.g., MA covered_by V). Prevents duplicate deep dives on duopoly pairs.
4. **`above_budget_rank` (integer, nullable)** — Rank among names demoted from deep_dive for budget reasons. Enables clean prioritization for the next cycle without relying on reason_for_action text parsing.
